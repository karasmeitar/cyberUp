WHEN S.O.L.I.D MEETS ANGULAR | Shiri Haim
The main notion of this talk is to introduce the S.O.L.I.D principles within the context of front-end components. We will discuss the reasons for implementing the S.O.L.I.D principles in our renew web applications as a comparison to our starting point. We will suggest guidelines based on our interpretation of S.O.L.I.D in the front-end world. We will enrich the discussion by supplying various practical examples regarding the guidelines implementation.

CYBERARK'S MICRO FRONT-END ARCHITECTURE | Meitar Karas
